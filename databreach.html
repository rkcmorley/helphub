Experian Data Breach Response Service

With a dedicated global data breach response team and more than 10 years providing pre and post breach resolution services to thousands of organisations, Experian have the flexibility, technology and expertise to deliver a comprehensive service to help you assess and respond quickly.

We will discreetly help you handle each data breach incident whether it affects hundreds or thousands of individuals and help you build a solid foundation for managing reputational, financial damage and rebuild trust.

Who's it for?

Organisations that need to prepare for or quickly respond in the event of a data breach.

Key features

  • Pre Breach Assessment
  • Incident Response Service
  • Customer Support
  • Reporting Suite

How it works

Once you become aware of a data breach you will want to quickly assess the situation. Utilising our proven expertise we will ensure a comprehensive data breach plan is created. Your dedicated service manager will help you through the process to ensure both customers’ and employees’ peace of mind is quickly restored by delivering timely, pre-defined communications. Once individuals are informed Experian’s call centre operation will provide dedicated support and guidance to affected individuals at this critical time and so they can quickly gain access to real-time credit and web monitoring solutions.
